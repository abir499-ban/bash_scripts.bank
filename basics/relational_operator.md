## Arithmetic Comparison Operators in Bash

Used for **numbers only**.

| Operator | Meaning                      |
|----------|------------------------------|
| `-eq`    | Equal                        |
| `-ne`    | Not equal                    |
| `-lt`    | Less than                    |
| `-le`    | Less than or equal           |
| `-gt`    | Greater than                 |
| `-ge`    | Greater than or equal        |
